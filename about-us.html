About Jermaine Lee Physical Therapy

Our philosophy is to deliver individualized care through evidence-based practices and expert clinical staff to promote better physical health.

At Jermaine Lee Physical Therapy, we don’t just deliver evidence-based care. We deliver care that is lived, tested, and inspired by real recovery.

I’m a Doctor of Physical Therapy and a former Division I football player who trained and competed at the highest level. After my Pro Day with both NFL and CFL teams, I was drafted to play professionally in the Canadian Football League for the Edmonton Elks.

My identity was rooted in athletic performance until July 2022, when a hiking accident changed everything. That day, an awkward landing led to a rare, life-altering injury: bilateral quadriceps tendon rupture.

The accident came at the price of my love for fitness and outdoor activity, something that had always defined who I was. I never imagined I would have to learn to walk again. But I did. And that journey from complete immobility to recovery reshaped not just my body, but my purpose.

I went from provider to patient, unable to walk, dependent on others, and forced to rebuild from scratch. That experience didn’t just deepen my knowledge of physical therapy—it deepened my empathy. It taught me how isolating and painful recovery can be, and how powerful the right care truly is.

That experience now shapes everything we do at Jermaine Lee Physical Therapy, from our personalized care plans to our unwavering commitment to helping each patient feel seen, supported, and empowered.

Today, my team and I combine expert clinical care, individualized treatment, and real-world experience to help others rise, just as I had to. Whether you are recovering from injury, surgery, or chronic pain, you are not alone. You are with someone who has lived it and come back stronger.
